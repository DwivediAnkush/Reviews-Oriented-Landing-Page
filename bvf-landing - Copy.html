D.El.Ed (JBT)
Diploma in Elementary Education
2 Years Online Mode Govt. Approved

D.El.Ed (also known as JBT – Junior Basic Teacher) is a 2-year diploma program that qualifies you to teach Classes 1–8 in government schools. It is mandatory for primary school teachers under RTE Act. Perfect for 12th pass aspirants.

Eligibility: 12th Pass (50%+)
Primary School Teaching
RTE Act Compliant
Practical Training
CTET Eligibility
Affordable Fee Structure

NTT
Nursery Teacher Training
1 Year Flexible Timing High Demand

NTT is a 1-2 year certificate program that trains you to teach and nurture children aged 2.5 to 6 years in nursery, LKG, and UKG classes. It is ideal for compassionate individuals who love working with young children. Private school demand for NTT teachers is growing rapidly.

Eligibility: 12th Pass
Early Childhood Education
Montessori Techniques
Creative Teaching Methods
Child Psychology Basics
Fast Placement Opportunities

2 Years Postgraduate Research Focus
